About me

I’m Fernanda! A UX/UI Designer with a background in Occupational Therapy. Based in San Francisco. I love Sourdough bread, plants, and colors. I’ve made a life out of thinking (sometimes thinking too much) about how people interact and connect with their environment and how can I improve their goal to be achieved and inclusive.

  • I truly believe in accessible design.
  • I love seeing user behavior based on interface interaction.
  • I'm thrilled to do a design that can be implemented for all needs.
